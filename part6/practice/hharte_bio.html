  Herbert R. Hartel, Jr.: Bio

Herbert R. Hartel, Jr. is substitute instructor of art history at John Jay College of Criminal Justice, CUNY. He has also taught at Fordham University, Pratt Institute, Baruch College and York College, CUNY. A doctoral candidate in the Ph.D. Program in Art History at the CUNY Graduate Center, he is currently working on his dissertation, which is a monographic study of the New Mexico abstract painter Raymond Jonson. He is a former intern at the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

He can be reached via email at hartel70@aol.com.

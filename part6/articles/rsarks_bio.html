Raphy Sarkissian: Bio

Raphy Sarkissian is an artist who is currently pursuing his Ph.D. in art history at The Graduate Center. Following his M.A. in Studio Arts at New York University, he exhibited /copies/ of Sherrie Levine's monochromes of Yves Klein, which he titled through excerpts from the essay "Bachelors" of Professor Rosalind Krauss. During summer 2000 he was a guest professor at State University of New York at New Paltz. He is thankful to Professor Laurie Schneider Adams for her close reading of, and numerous suggestions for, earlier drafts of this essay.

He can be reached via email at rsarkissi@aol.com.

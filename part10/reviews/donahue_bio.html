Editor's Note
 
by Mary Donahue
 
 

Mary Donahue, Ph.D. teaches at Troy State University Florida Region and the University of Miami. Her expertise is the development of women’s garment design in the United States; and special interest is the Pre-Raphaelite and Aesthetic Movement in England. Her current projects involve investigations into national identity in the production of American fashion in the late 19th and early 20th centuries as well as Rossetti’s late paintings of women.
